About

My name is Megan. I’m a creative developer with digital agency experience, building clean, user-focused websites. I specialise in bespoke WordPress sites that are visually engaging, easy to use, and built to perform. Whether you need something brand new or a refresh, I focus on building with your business goals in mind.

Services

  1. Overview

    Custom WordPress websites built from existing designs or clear brand direction. Developed to be fast, reliable and easy to manage.

    What does it include?

    • Custom WordPress development
    • Pixel-perfect front-end build from designs (Figma, etc.)
    • Responsive development (mobile, tablet, desktop)
    • Clean, maintainable code
    • CMS setup for easy content editing
    • Performance optimisation
    • Basic technical SEO setup
    • Forms and functionality integration
    • Launch support

    Who it's for?

    • Businesses with an existing design or brand direction
    • Service-based businesses looking for a reliable, scalable website
    • Projects that need clean, high-quality development
    • Teams working with a designer who need a developer to bring it to life

    The process

    Discovery

    Understand your goals, content and design direction

    Preparation

    Review designs and assets, define structure if needed

    Development

    Front-end build and WordPress integration

    Refinement

    Feedback and adjustments

    Launch

    Go live and handover

  2. Overview

    Development-focused improvements to your existing website to enhance performance, usability and overall quality.

    What does it include?

    • Front-end fixes and enhancements
    • Mobile optimisation
    • Speed and performance improvements
    • Layout adjustments (within existing design)
    • WordPress fixes and improvements
    • CMS restructuring for easier content management
    • Bug fixing and cleanup
    • Accessibility improvements

    Who it's for?

    • Existing websites that feel outdated or underperforming
    • Businesses not ready for a full rebuild
    • Sites with technical issues or poor mobile experience
    • WordPress sites that are difficult to manage or maintain

    The process

    Audit

    Review current site (technical and UX issues)

    Recommendations

    Confirm clear, prioritised improvements

    Implementation

    Apply updates and fixes

    Review

    Final checks and tweaks

  3. Overview

    Ongoing support to keep your website running smoothly, up to date and performing as your business evolves.

    What does it include?

    • Content updates
    • Technical maintenance
    • Bug fixes
    • Performance monitoring
    • Small front-end improvements
    • WordPress support
    • Ongoing guidance and advice

    Who it's for?

    • Businesses who want ongoing help without hiring in-house
    • Websites that need regular updates or maintenance
    • Teams who want a reliable developer to work with
    • Growing businesses that need flexibility

    The process

    Setup

    Review your site and understand your requirements

    Requests

    Flexible updates as needed

    Suggestions to enhance performance and usability over time
